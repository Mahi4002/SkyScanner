Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I book a flight?

To book a flight, simply search for your desired destination, select your flight options, and proceed with payment. You'll receive a confirmation via email.

2. Can I change or cancel my booking?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your booking through the 'My Bookings' section. Changes may be subject to fees, depending on the airline's policy.

3. How can I request a refund?

Refund requests can be initiated from your account dashboard. Refunds are processed according to the airline's policy and may take a few days to reflect.

4. What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and online payment systems such as PayPal. Ensure your payment information is accurate to avoid issues.

5. Do I need to print my ticket?

Most airlines accept mobile boarding passes, so printing your ticket isn't necessary. Check your email or the 'My Bookings' section for the digital ticket.

Helpful Tips

  • Check your email regularly for booking confirmation and updates.
  • Always double-check your flight details before finalizing a booking.
  • Ensure your travel documents (passport, visa, etc.) are up to date.
  • Arrive at the airport at least 2 hours before your flight for smooth check-in.
